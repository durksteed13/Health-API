Health API

About

+

We are entering an age where the advances in technology have exponentially increased the availability of data. Despite this abundance of information online, certain data can still be elusive, especially when it comes to our health. Many healthcare professionals, students, and consumers could benefit from access to comprehensive databases without having extensive coding ability.

HealthAPI is a website that allows for easy and intuitive access to health data. Take advantage of the numerous APIs on our platform to fetch data in a simple and easy process. By creating an account, you will be able to save specific searches and have even quicker access to your relevant data.

Regardless of whether you are a healthcare professional or consumer, we hope that this site can be the bridge between you and health data. Thank you for visiting HealthAPI.


General Questions

+

How do I use this site?

Our website allows you to retrieve specific health-related data without having to independently learn how to make API requests. By using our interface, users are able to easily retrieve data from their desired API and specific search parameters.

Who should be using this site?

Anyone! With our simple layout and intuitive design, our service gives you access to many medical APIs. HealthAPI is simply a bridge between any person interested in health data and API endpoints.

What are the benefits to creating an account?

Creating an account allows you save specific API searches for future reference and quicker access. Particular users in the field of pharmacy, nutrition, biostatistics, etc. may be keen to take advantage of this feature.
