FAQs

How do I use this site?

Our website allows you to retrieve specific health-related data without having to independently learn how to ping an API. By using the dropdown selections with autocomplete suggestions, users are able to easily select for their desired API and specific search parameters.

Who should be using this site?

Anyone! With our simple layout and intuitive design, our service gives you access to a multitude of medical APIs in an efficient and lucid manner. In an era where emerging diseases have never been more relevant, accurate and applicable data can actually be increasingly elusive at times. Thus, health professionals may also be the beneficiary as we provide instant access to the data within a number of APIs.

What are the benefits to creating an account?

Creating an account allows you to maintain a record of previous API searches for future reference. Particular users in the field of pharmacy, nutrition, biostatistics, etc. may be keen to take advantage of this feature.
